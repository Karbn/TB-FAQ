Email and Account Activation

Q: Why do I need a real email address on my account, and why do I need to "activate" it?

A: We require a real email address on your account as a form of security. For example if your account is ever stolen, we can use the email address to get your account back. By activating the email address we can verify that it is a real address. Also, if you don't activate your email you wont be able to trade or post.



Q: How do I change the email address on my account?

A: All of your account settings can be changed in the UserCP (User Control Panel), just go there and on the left hand side select "Edit Email & Password".



Q: How do I activate my account?

A: Once an activation email has been sent, just go to your email inbox and look for a message from Toribash, and click the link inside the email. The message may end up in your junk/spam email, so check that as well.



Q: I didn't get an activation email, how do I get a new one sent?

A: You can request a new email here.



Q: But what if I still didn't receive an email?

A: If that is the case you need to contact an Admin, which you can find here. They are easy recognizable as they have a red name. Even if your account is not activated, you can contact an admin by posting in Technical Support or by visiting the #support IRC channel
