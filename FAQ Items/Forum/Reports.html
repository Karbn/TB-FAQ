Reports

Q: What can I report for?

A: You can report anything that is rule breaking, insulting or illegal. Take a look at the board's rules thread and the general forum rules. Before you submit a report, to be sure that it really is rule breaking.



Q: Where do i report?

Q: Can I Report for XYZ?

A: The things you should not report are:

  • - Spelling errors. Annoying, but not against the rules.
  • - Stupidity. Again, annoying, but not against the rules. If someone is being stupid, the best response is usually to just ignore it.
  • - Small generally not particularly offensive things, such as saying "ass" in a post.

Make Sure Your Report Is Intelligible:

THIS IS IMPORTANT! Mods need to be able to read and understand the problem.


It may take a while for mods to take action, and all reports get sent to the Super Moderators. Remember to include all relevant details in the report. Abusing the report feature can result in infractions or bans.



Q: How do I report something from ingame?

A: It is slightly more complicated to report any rule breaking that happened in the game.

To report something from ingame, you (almost) always need to screenshot the offense. Then upload the picture to some image hosting website. But Imgur is recommended.

Before Reporting, READ THE STICKIES.


Keep these things in mind, when you want to report something that happened ingame.

Once you have uploaded it to an image hosting website, link it to a Super Moderator in a PM or create a thread about it in the General Reports board.

While flaming is an offense, is it really worth reporting someone if they called you a buttface?

DO NOT REPORT WITHOUT EVIDENCE. The mods won't ban people just because you ask them to.

When reporting, try to keep it readable. This saves the Super Moderators (Blue name) a lot of time and effort when helping you. Check your PMs regularly to see if they replied.

Again, make sure they're actually breaking a rule with their behavior.
